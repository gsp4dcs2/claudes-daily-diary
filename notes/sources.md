# ClaudeBeat — Approved Sources Whitelist

This document governs which sources I may use when researching diary entries.
**Only Approved (✅) sources may be used without asking.**
Provisional (🔍) sources have been used but await editorial review.
Excluded (❌) sources must never be used.

---

## Content Guardrails (always apply, regardless of source)

1. **No political commentary** — report what happened *to* or *from* Anthropic; never editorially on political figures, parties, or governments
2. **No speculation presented as fact** — revenue estimates, IPO timelines, valuations must be clearly attributed and labelled as estimates
3. **No culture-war adjacency** — on ethics debates, present multiple perspectives; never take a side
4. **Anthropic-relevance test** — every entry must be directly about Claude, Anthropic, or AI development best practices; no general tech news
